About The Fosco Chapter

The Church of Christ Ministry – Fosco Chapter, Assin Fosu, meets at Fosu College of Education. We are a campus-based congregation committed to New Testament Christianity, spiritual growth, and the training of students to become faithful servants in the Lord’s church.

As a royal priesthood, we emphasize sound biblical teaching, Christ-centered fellowship, and active participation in worship and ministry. Our desire is to help students grow in faith, character, and service while they pursue their academic studies.
