About The Fosco Chapter

The Church of Christ Ministry – Fosco Chapter, Assin Fosu, meets at Fosu College of Education.

We are a campus-based congregation committed to New Testament Christianity, spiritual growth, and the training of students to become faithful servants in the Lord’s church.
