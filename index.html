About us

The Churches of Christ in Ghana are autonomous congregations of believers seeking to follow the pattern of the New Testament church. We emphasize biblical teaching, congregational leadership by elders where possible, a cappella worship, and the unity of the church in Christ.

This website serves as a central place to discover congregations, upcoming events, and scriptural resources to strengthen faith and fellowship.
